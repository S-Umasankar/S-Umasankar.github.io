  • Savvy direct semantic for palsied [Aug 2020 – Nov 2020]
    • Sporadically, we encounter middle aged people affected by hemiparesis (hemi – half; paresis - paralysis). The major difficulties faced by the people affected by hemiparesis is the inability to convey their basic needs through oral communication (if they become orally impaired). The only possible way of communicating their basic need is through gestures. The objective of this project is to convert the gesture given by the patient into an equivalent speech output. Deep Learning models are being deployed to recognize the crux of their gestures.
